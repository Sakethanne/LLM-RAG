Effective Time Management Strategies to Adopt:

Identify most productive time of day.
Create a prioritized to-do list.
Break tasks into smaller, manageable chunks.
Use time-blocking techniques to allocate specific time slots for tasks.
Minimize multitasking to maintain focus and efficiency.
Utilize technology tools such as calendar apps and task management software.
Delegate tasks when possible to free up time for high-priority activities.
Practice the 2-minute rule: if a task takes less than 2 minutes, do it immediately.
Set deadlines for tasks to create a sense of urgency and accountability.
Regularly review and adjust your schedule to accommodate changes and optimize productivity.
Practice self-discipline and stick to your planned schedule, avoiding procrastination.
